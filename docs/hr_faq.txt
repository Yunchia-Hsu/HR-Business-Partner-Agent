LEAVE QUESTIONS

Q: How many days of annual leave do I get?
A: Full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis.

Q: Can I carry over unused annual leave?
A: Yes, you can carry over up to 10 days to the following year. Carried-over leave must be used by March 31 or it will be forfeited.

Q: How do I apply for annual leave?
A: Submit your request through the HR portal at hr.iceye.fi at least 2 weeks in advance. Your manager will approve based on team availability.

Q: How many sick days do I have?
A: You are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year. Sick leave does not carry over to the next year.

Q: Do I need a doctor's note for sick leave?
A: A medical certificate is required if you are absent for more than 3 consecutive days. For shorter absences, notify your manager on day one and submit through the portal when you return.

Q: How long is parental leave?
A: Primary caregivers receive 6 months of fully paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks of fully paid parental leave. This applies to birth, adoption, and surrogacy.

Q: How far in advance do I need to notify HR about parental leave?
A: At least 8 weeks before your intended start date. You can start leave up to 4 weeks before the expected due date.

Q: Will I have my job when I return from parental leave?
A: Yes. ICEYE guarantees your same or equivalent role upon return from parental leave.

Q: How many days of bereavement leave do I get?
A: 5 days for immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling). 3 days for other close relatives (grandparent, in-law).

Q: Can I take study leave?
A: Yes, up to 5 days of paid study leave per year for degrees or certifications relevant to your role. Requires manager and HR approval at least 4 weeks in advance.

BENEFITS QUESTIONS

Q: What healthcare does ICEYE provide?
A: Comprehensive occupational healthcare through Terveystalo, including GP visits, specialist referrals, and up to 10 free mental health therapy sessions per year.

Q: Is there support for mental health?
A: Yes. You have access to up to 10 free therapy sessions per year and a confidential Employee Assistance Programme (EAP) for counselling support.

Q: What is the sports and culture benefit?
A: ICEYE contributes 400 EUR per year via ePassi, which you can use for gym memberships, sports equipment, cinema, theatre, and cultural events.

Q: How much is the learning budget?
A: 1,500 EUR per employee per year. It can be used for courses, conferences, books, certifications, and workshops. Submit a request to your manager before booking.

Q: Does ICEYE offer LinkedIn Learning?
A: Yes, all employees have free access to LinkedIn Learning.

Q: How many days can I work remotely?
A: Up to 3 days per week, subject to agreement with your manager. Core hours are 10:00–15:00 Helsinki time.

Q: Can I work from another country?
A: Yes, international remote work is allowed for up to 30 days per year with prior HR approval.

Q: Is there a home office allowance?
A: New employees receive a one-time home office setup allowance of 500 EUR.

Q: What is the lunch benefit?
A: Up to 13 EUR per working day via Edenred, usable at participating restaurants near ICEYE offices.

Q: Is there a commuting benefit?
A: Yes, up to 100 EUR per month towards public transport costs.

Q: What equipment will I get?
A: A company laptop on day one (choice of macOS or Windows). Additional equipment like monitors can be requested through the IT helpdesk.

Q: When is the salary review?
A: Salaries are reviewed annually every February, based on individual performance, market benchmarking, and company performance.

CAREER DEVELOPMENT QUESTIONS

Q: How often are performance reviews?
A: Twice a year — in June (mid-year check-in) and December (full review including promotion and compensation decisions).

Q: How does the promotion process work?
A: Promotions are considered during the December review. You need to have been performing consistently at the next level for at least 6 months. Your manager initiates the case and it is reviewed by the HR committee.

Q: What if my promotion is not approved?
A: You will receive written feedback and a clear development plan with specific milestones to work towards.

Q: What are the engineering career levels?
A: Associate, Engineer, Senior Engineer, Staff Engineer, Principal Engineer. Each level has defined competencies covering technical skills, impact, and collaboration. The full framework is at hr.iceye.fi/careers.

Q: How do I prepare for a career development conversation with my manager?
A: Reflect on what you have achieved, what you want to grow in, and what support you need. Come with specific questions, for example: "What skills do I need to reach the next level?" or "Are there projects I could take on to stretch my abilities?"

Q: How do I prepare for a performance review?
A: Review your goals, gather concrete examples using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result), and be honest about areas for improvement. Agree on next steps in writing after the meeting.

Q: Is there a mentoring programme?
A: Yes. ICEYE runs a voluntary internal mentoring programme. Pairs meet monthly for 6 months. Apply through the HR portal.

Q: Can I move to a different team internally?
A: Yes, after 12 months in your current role you can apply for internal transfers. Open positions are posted on the HR portal before being listed externally.

Q: How should I give feedback to a colleague?
A: Use the observation + impact + suggestion model. Example: "When you did X (observation), it had Y effect (impact). Could you try Z instead? (suggestion)" Be specific, prompt, and constructive.
